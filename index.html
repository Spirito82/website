With me by your side, you can relax knowing your projects are in expert hands

You are in a perpetual race against the clock, striving to support client growth with strategic, compliant, and accurate communications.

You face challenges with fluctuating workloads, often peaking during campaigns. Your projects are diverse, and some require niche skills or knowledge that your in-house team may lack. Your clients demand quick turnarounds and the ability to pivot strategies rapidly, sometimes pushing your team beyond its limits.

Balancing multiple deadlines with varied client profiles and portfolios while maintaining high-quality output is exhausting. This pressure is compounded when new or special projects demand immediate attention, leaving you with little time for personal life and well-being.

Whether you are in an advertising, education, publishing, or marketing agency, finding a partner who can step in and support you is not negotiable.

However, finding the right freelance partner can feel as daunting as finding a life partner.

I understand this struggle firsthand — I’ve had to pick up and redo projects after agencies worked with freelancers who didn’t meet expectations.

Medical writers need a unique blend of skills, including tailored deliverables on time, in-depth scientific specialization, business acumen, and the ability to understand agency-client relationships and unique value propositions.

The wrong fit can lead to a bad impression or even lost clients.

But the right partner does exist!

Imagine a collaborator who not only meets but exceeds your expectations.

With a PhD and postdoctoral experience, working across three continents and various therapeutic areas, I bring a deep well of knowledge and critical thinking to every project. As a new immigrant in the post-pandemic recession, I built my business during a challenging postpartum period, so I understand resilience and reliability like no one else.

Having worked with numerous agencies, I recognize the importance of delivering value to your clients. My track record speaks for itself: I’ve never missed a deadline, familiar with project and product life cycles, and clients consistently return with more work.

I offer more than just clear, compliant medical writing—I provide critical and creative insights, proactive solutions, and a unique perspective.

If you seek a mature writer with world experience, client experience, and experience in cutting-edge research, I am your partner.

Let’s turn your challenges into success stories and profit. With me on your team, you can relax knowing your projects are in expert hands.
